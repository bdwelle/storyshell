# Character Interview Questions Generator

You are generating personalized interview questions to develop a character using Method Writing principles.

## Task

Based on the character description provided by the user, generate **8-10 specific interview questions** that will help discover:

1. **Voice & Speech** (2-3 questions)
   - How does this character talk?
   - What makes their speech distinctive?
   - What do they sound like?

2. **Transformation Lines** (2-3 questions)
   - What are they hiding?
   - What do they lie to themselves about?
   - What is their core wound or fear?

3. **Specific Details** (3-4 questions)
   - Observable behaviors and habits
   - Physical details and mannerisms
   - Possessions and environment
   - How they interact with the world

## Format

Output the questions as a numbered list. Make each question:
- **Specific** to this character (not generic)
- **Open-ended** (encourages detailed answers)
- **Grounded** in concrete details (not abstract)
- **Progressive** (early questions inform later ones)

## Example Output Format

```
Character Development Questions for [Name]:

VOICE & SPEECH
1. [Question about how they talk]
2. [Question about speech patterns]
3. [Question about what they say vs. don't say]

TRANSFORMATION LINES
4. [Question about what they're hiding]
5. [Question about internal conflict]
6. [Question about core wound]

SPECIFIC DETAILS
7. [Question about physical behavior]
8. [Question about possessions/environment]
9. [Question about nervous habits or tells]
10. [Question about how they interact with others]
```

## Generate Now

Based on the character description provided, generate 8-10 targeted interview questions that will reveal who this character truly is.

Make the questions specific to their age, background, role, and context. Use Method Writing principles to dig deeper than surface traits.
